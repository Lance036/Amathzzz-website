About the Project

Amathzzz is a research project by STEM 12 - Nikola Tesla students from Bacoor Elementary School (S.Y. 2023–2024), as part of their subject: Inquiries, Investigations, and Immersion. The project explores the potential of AI chatbots in enhancing math education through hands-on experimentation and real-world feedback.

Lance Perus

Developer of Amathzzz and a student researcher of STEM 12 - Nikola Tesla. Lance designed and built the website within four days as part of the team's research initiative. His goal is to graduate with a meaningful contribution to AI and education.
